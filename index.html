Infrastructure & Services

Immunology Testing

State-of-the-art immunology and serology testing utilizing the Roche cobas e411 analyzer to support reliable diagnosis and monitoring of immune disorders, hormonal imbalances, allergic conditions, autoimmune diseases, and infectious illnesses.

Immunology Tests Include:

  • Autoimmune profile tests
  • Hormone assays
  • Tumor marker testing
  • Allergy testing
  • Infectious disease serology

Biochemistry Laboratory Services

Comprehensive biochemistry testing for routine health checkups, preventive care, and chronic disease monitoring.

Biochemistry Tests Include:

  • Liver function test (LFT)
  • Kidney function test (KFT)
  • Lipid profile
  • Blood glucose & HbA1c
  • Electrolytes and cardiac markers

Microbiology Testing Services

Accurate microbiology testing for the detection of bacterial, fungal, and mycobacterial infections.

Microbiology Services Include:

  • Culture & sensitivity testing
  • Urine, blood, stool, and wound cultures
  • Fungal and mycobacteriology testing

Histopathology & Cytology Services

Expert histopathology services with detailed tissue examination and pathological diagnosis by experienced pathologists.

Histopathology Services Include:

  • Surgical pathology
  • Biopsy analysis
  • FNAC & cytology
  • Special stains and IHC (where applicable)
